.. py:function:: match_CCCG_on(src_arr, col_name)

   Get a list of CCCGs based on single coupling factor, meaning the CCCGs are grouped because
   they have at least one shared variable of the given coupling factor

   :param src_arr: Component table with expanded coupling factors
   :type src_arr: pd.DataFrame
   :param col_name: coupling factor, Function\_, Input\_ or Design\_
   :type col_name: str

   :returns: list of CCCGs based on single coupling factor, arranged in the order \*_1, \*_2, \*_3, etc. where \* represents the coupling factor
   :rtype: list
